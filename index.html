About Me

I am a passionate industrial product designer with almost six years of experience in the field. However, my love for video games led me to pursue a new career in game development. I have been learning C# and Unity on my own and have taken up a game development course to gain more knowledge in the field. These experiences have allowed me to merge my design skills with a new set of technical abilities.
As a multidisciplinary team player, I bring my design expertise and creativity to game development to create engaging experiences for players. I firmly believe that storytelling and strong visualization are key to the success of any product, whether physical or virtual.
I am enthusiastic about learning and improving myself, and I enjoy exploring new tools and techniques to enhance my skills. During my free time, I enjoy immersing myself in video games and exploring the intricate worlds crafted by other developers. With my multidisciplinary background and passion for game development, I look forward to contributing to the creation of memorable and enjoyable experiences for players.
