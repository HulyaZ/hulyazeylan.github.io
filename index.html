About Me

I'm a Unity Generalist and 3D Artist, blending creativity and technical skill at CODEO. My foundation in Industrial Design, recognized for its strong emphasis on problem-solving, has enabled me to seamlessly integrate a design-thinking approach with hands-on skills in C# and Unity. In my approach to projects, I bring not only my technical expertise and creative vision but also my strong collaboration skills, fast learning ability, and perseverance. These qualities enable me to work effectively within multidisciplinary teams, ensuring project success from concept to completion.

While my professional focus lies in crafting immersive experiences through design and coding, my passions extend far beyond the office walls. Outside of work, I find inspiration in the captivating narratives, unique art styles, and innovative designs of games, particularly in the bold endeavours of indie developers who fearlessly explore uncharted territories.

Beyond gaming, I'm drawn to the art of puzzle-making and crafting, where I channel my creativity into tangible forms. Whether I'm exploring digital worlds or engaging in hands-on creativity, I'm always seeking new avenues for exploration and expression.

In both my professional and personal pursuits, I strive to bridge the gap between creativity and technology, crafting experiences that engage, inspire, and transport. From intricate game design to puzzle-making and crafting, I'm dedicated to bringing ideas to life in meaningful and memorable ways.
